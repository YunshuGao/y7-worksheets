TermDefinitionUse in a sentence (Extension)
Truss A rigid framework of members using triangles to distribute load
Member A single structural element — one craft stick
Joint A point where two or more members connect — a glued overlap
Cross-brace A short member connecting two parallel trusses to prevent twisting
Deck The flat surface on top where load sits — the road surface
Alignment Making sure both side trusses are identical in shape and size

Activity 3: PVA Glue Technique (Folio p. 77)

PVA Gluing Tips:

  • Apply a thin, even layer on BOTH surfaces
  • Press firmly for 10 seconds
  • Clamp with rubber bands while drying
  • Common mistake: More glue ≠ stronger! Excess glue weakens joints.

Activity 4: Build First Triangle — Guided Practice (Folio p. 77)

Follow these steps to build your first triangle. This is the building block of your truss.

StepInstructionDone? Notes
1Lay 3 sticks in a triangle shape on a flat surface
2Overlap sticks by 1 cm at each vertex (corner)
3Apply thin PVA glue to BOTH overlapping surfaces
4Press and hold for 10 seconds at each joint
5Clamp joints with rubber bands

💡 Tips for Matching Trusses

  • Count the same number of members and triangles
