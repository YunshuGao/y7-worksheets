Key Vocabulary

Standard: Fill in the definition.  |  Support: Definitions shown in grey — read and copy into your folio.  |  Extension: Use each term in a sentence.

Term Definition Use in a sentence (Extension)
Technology Any tool, system, or process created to solve a problem
Design The process of creating something to solve a problem or meet a need
Designer A person who plans and creates solutions to problems
Innovation A new idea, method, or product that improves on what existed before
Solution An answer to a problem; something that fixes or improves a situation
Problem-solving The process of finding answers to difficulties or challenges
Focus area One of the 4 main topic areas in Technology Mandatory
