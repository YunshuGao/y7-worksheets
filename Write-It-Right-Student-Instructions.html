✏️ Write It Right

Year 7 Technology — Academic Writing Program

Open this link in your browser:
yunshugao.github.io/y7-worksheets/Write-It-Right.html
  1. Open Google Chrome and type the link above.
  2. Enter your full name at the top of the page.
  3. Start with the Diagnostic — match words, answer 3 questions, write 2 sentences.
  4. Click "See My Results" to get your starting level.
  5. Work through Module 1, 2, 3, 4... at your own pace.
  6. Read the coloured help boxes — they give you hints and sentence starters.
  7. Before packing up: Click the green 💾 Save / Load button, then Copy Save Code, and paste it into a Google Doc or email it to yourself.

⚠️ Rules

  • No ChatGPT or AI tools — write in your own words
  • Do NOT click Reset — it deletes all your work
  • Save your code before packing up — you'll need it next lesson if you use a different computer
💡 Tip: You'll see some words you haven't learned yet (like isometric). That's fine — the program explains them. You'll cover them properly in future lessons.
