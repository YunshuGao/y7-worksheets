Technical lettering ensures engineering drawings are clear and readable for everyone.

#RuleWhy it matters

2Keep all letters the same height
3Space letters evenly

Scale Calculations (Folio pp. 52–55)

Model → Real (finding real size)

Real = Model × Scale factor

e.g. 5 cm at 1:10 → 5 × 10 = 50 cm

Real → Model (finding model size)

Model = Real ÷ Scale factor

e.g. 3 m at 1:20 → 300 cm ÷ 20 = 15 cm

🔵 Support — Scale Calculation Steps

Follow these steps for EVERY scale problem:

  1. What unit? Convert metres to centimetres first
  2. Model or real? Am I finding the model (divide) or real size (multiply)?
  3. Calculate: Do the maths
  4. Sense check: Does my answer seem reasonable?
