7 Engineering Forces Round 5

Every structure must deal with these three forces.

⬇️⬆️
COMPRESSION
Pushing / squashing force — pushes things together
Example: Pillars of a building being pushed down by weight above
↔️
TENSION
Pulling / stretching force — pulls things apart
Example: Cables on a suspension bridge being pulled tight
⬇️
GRAVITY
Downward force pulling everything towards Earth
Example: A ball falling, weight pushing down on a bridge deck

Match the force to the scenario:

A rubber band being stretched →
An apple falling from a tree →
Legs of a chair supporting you →
