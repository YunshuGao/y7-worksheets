🔵 Support — Building Tips
  1. Roll paper into tubes — tubes are stronger than flat sheets
  2. Use triangles — triangles are the strongest shape because they cannot collapse
  3. Make a wide base — a wider base makes structures more stable
  4. Fold paper to make it thicker and stronger
