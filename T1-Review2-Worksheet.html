3 Scale Calculations Round 2

Remember: Model → Real: multiply | Real → Model: divide | Always convert metres to centimetres first!

🧠 Formulas: Real size = Model × Scale factor  |  Model size = Real ÷ Scale factor  |  1 m = 100 cm
