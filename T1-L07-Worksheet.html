Key Vocabulary

Standard: Fill in the definition.  |  Support: Definitions shown in grey — read and copy into your folio.  |  Extension: Use each term in a sentence.

TermDefinitionUse in a sentence (Extension)
Design process A series of steps designers follow to create a solution to a problem
Identify & Define Stage 1: Work out what the problem is and what needs to be solved
Research & Plan Stage 2: Gather information and plan your approach
Produce & Implement Stage 3: Build or make the design
Test & Evaluate Stage 4: Check if the design works and suggest improvements
Iteration Repeating the process to improve the design each time
