Activity 3: Cross-Bracing (Folio p. 79)

Cross-braces connect the two side trusses to prevent twisting. Follow these steps:

StepInstructionDone? Notes
1Measure the gap between the two trusses
2Cut or select cross-brace sticks to the correct length
3Glue both ends of each cross-brace
4Press firmly and hold
5Space cross-braces every 3–4 cm along the bridge
6Check for twisting — bridge should sit flat and square
🛠
Activity 4: Install Deck (Folio p. 80)

The deck is the flat surface where the load will sit.

  • Lay sticks flat across the top, perpendicular to the bridge length
  • Glue each deck stick to the cross-braces below
  • Place sticks edge to edge — no gaps

Activity 5: Reinforce Weak Joints (Folio p. 80)

Inspect every joint on your bridge. Strengthen any weak points.

  • Check every joint — are any loose or cracked?
  • Add extra glue to weak joints
  • Double up critical spots (add an extra stick alongside)
  • Focus on the bottom chord and end supports
📏
Activity 6: Final Alignment Check (Folio p. 80)
